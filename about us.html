About Us

We Deliver Home and Abroad

SEMA FOODS, established on August 1st, 2022, envisions becoming the world's leading food processing and packaging company built on sincerity, trust, and communication. Operating in Lagos and Ibadan, Nigeria, they specialize in sourcing the best foodstuffs, ensuring neat packaging under hygienic conditions. Offering affordable prices in wholesale and retail, SEMA FOODS serves over 300 people through various programs. Recognizing the challenges customers face in purchasing foodstuffs and the rising cost of food in Nigeria, SEMA FOODS is committed to providing solutions. They are dedicated to addressing issues such as delivery delays and the overseas longing for African foods. The company aims to offer customers a home away from home satisfaction by ensuring the accessibility and quality of African foodstuffs, along with meeting specified delivery deadlines. Overall, SEMA FOODS stands as a promising venture focused on transforming the food industry and meeting the diverse needs of its customers.
